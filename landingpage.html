FAQS

  What is TimeTabler?

TimeTabler is a timetable generator platform that allows users to automatically create, edit, customize, and download timetables, making scheduling easy and efficient for schools, colleges, and organizations.

 How does the timetable generation work?

Our system uses dynamic algorithms to generate optimized timetables based on inputs such as faculty availability, class schedules, subject preferences, and weekly hours, saving you the hassle of manual scheduling.

 Can I customize my generated timetable?

Yes, you can easily edit and customize your generated timetable. The platform allows you to adjust class timings, add or remove subjects, and make any changes you need to suit your preferences.

 Is it possible to download the timetables?

Absolutely! You can download your finalized timetables in both PDF and Excel formats, making it easy to share, print, or further customize your schedules as needed.

 Does TimeTabler manage absence of teachers?

Yes, TimeTabler has an absence management feature that allows you to track and adjust timetables based on teacher absences, ensuring minimal disruption to the schedule.

 Do I need to sign up to use TimeTabler?

Yes, users need to sign up and log in to access the timetable generation features and manage their timetables securely.

 Who can use TimeTabler?

TimeTabler is perfect for schools, colleges, universities, and organizations that need to manage class schedules, training sessions, or any other recurring events.

 Is TimeTabler free to use?

Yes, TimeTabler is completely free to use! You can access all features, including timetable generation, customization, and downloads, without any cost.

 Can I generate timetables for different departments or courses?

Yes, TimeTabler allows you to create and manage timetables for multiple departments or courses simultaneously, giving flexibility for various academic or organizational needs.

 Can TimeTabler handle complex timetables with multiple constraints?

Yes, TimeTabler is designed to handle complex schedules with multiple constraints, such as limited room availability, overlapping subjects, and specific faculty preferences, ensuring an optimized timetable for all situations.

 What inputs do I need to provide to generate a timetable?

You will need to input details like faculty names, subject expertise, class names, weekly hours per subject, and the available time slots or rooms. TimeTablerr will then automatically generate the timetable based on this data.

 How can I access my saved timetables?

After logging into your account, you can access all your saved timetables in the 'My Timetables' section, where you can view, edit, or download them at any time.

  Does TimeTabler support mobile devices?

Yes, TimeTabler is optimized for mobile devices, allowing you to generate, edit, and manage timetables on the go from any smartphone or tablet.

 How often can I edit or regenerate a timetable?

You can edit or regenerate your timetable as many times as needed until you're satisfied with the final version. There are no limits to adjustments or re-generations.

 How secure is my data on TimeTabler?

We take data security seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely, ensuring that only you have access to your timetables and personal information.
